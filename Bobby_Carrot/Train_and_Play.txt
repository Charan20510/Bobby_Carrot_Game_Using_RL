DQN (PYTORCH CNN)

Install dependency first:
pip install torch

1) TRAIN LEVELS 3, 4, 5 INDIVIDUALLY (SEPARATE MODELS)
python.exe .\train_dqn.py --levels 3 4 5 --individual-levels --episodes-per-level 6000 --max-steps 900 --checkpoint-dir .\dqn_checkpoints --report-every 50

Saved models:
.\dqn_checkpoints\dqn_level3_individual.pt
.\dqn_checkpoints\dqn_level4_individual.pt
.\dqn_checkpoints\dqn_level5_individual.pt

2) PLAY EACH DQN MODEL AUTONOMOUSLY

PLAY LEVEL 3:
python.exe .\train_dqn.py --play --map-kind normal --map-number 3 --model-path .\dqn_checkpoints\dqn_level3_individual.pt --play-episodes 3 --max-steps 900

PLAY LEVEL 4:
python.exe .\train_dqn.py --play --map-kind normal --map-number 4 --model-path .\dqn_checkpoints\dqn_level4_individual.pt --play-episodes 3 --max-steps 900

PLAY LEVEL 5:
python.exe .\train_dqn.py --play --map-kind normal --map-number 5 --model-path .\dqn_checkpoints\dqn_level5_individual.pt --play-episodes 3 --max-steps 900

3) OPTIONAL: SEQUENTIAL TRAINING ACROSS 3->4->5 (ONE MODEL, NO RESET)
python.exe .\train_dqn.py --levels 3 4 5 --episodes-per-level 6000 --max-steps 900 --checkpoint-dir .\dqn_checkpoints --model-path .\dqn_3_4_5_sequential.pt --report-every 50
