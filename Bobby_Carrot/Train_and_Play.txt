Resuming Training (From Best Checkpoint)

If training stops or disconnects, you can resume from exactly where you left off (maintaining episode count, optimizer state, and epsilon) using the --resume flag and pointing to your best checkpoint.

python train_dqn.py \
  --levels 7 \
  --episodes-per-level 10000 \
  --lr 3e-4 \
  --report-every 100 \
  --no-render \
  --resume \
  --model-path dqn_checkpoints/dqn_level7_sequential_best.pt

Note: Adjust the --levels, --episodes-per-level, and --model-path depending on the level you are currently training.
